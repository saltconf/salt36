## Transportation

Uber and Cabify are safe and quite cheap for short distances. You can also take
the bus or the subway (most of them can be paid with credit card). Here’s a map
with the closest important streets and public transportation options:

References

- 5 blocks from Rivadavia Avenue (Subway A station: PUAN; Sarmiento train station: CABALLITO; buses 1, 2, 8, 25, 49, 53, 55, 85, 86, 88, 96, 132, 136, 145, 163)
- 2 blocks from Directorio Avenue (buses 56, 97, 126, 134)

## Accommodation

The conference site is located in a residential area, so not many hotels are
available in the vicinity. Airbnb is always an option, or you may contact us 
for more recommendations.

We recommend staying in neighbourhoods close to Rivadavia Avenue (such as 
Caballito, Almagro, Monserrat, and Congreso), as they easy access to the A
subway. Some of these neighbourhoods are also close to the city downtown and
many tourist spots.

Neighborhoods like Recoleta, Palermo, and Villa Crespo are also an option, but 
keep in mind that they are somewhat distant from the conference site, and
commute may not be as straightforward.

We don’t recommend staying too far south from Rivadavia Avenue, as those
neighborhoods are generally not as safe as other options.
